001623change-request-proposals-crp-to-an-ogc-document
Hanging paragraph in clause
<clause id="change-request-proposals-crp-to-an-ogc-document" obligation="normative">
<title>Change Request Proposals (CRP) to an OGC document</title>
<p id="_ae6490a8-7bdb-c01c-70f5-901eebae70a6">At any time, any OGC member or non-member can submit a CRP. A CRP allows for the formal documentation of a proposed change to an existing, adopted OGC standard or abstract specification. The change could be an identified error (see <xref target="corrigendum-errata-changes-to-ogc-standards-full-standard"/>), an inconsistency, a requested enhancement, or a major proposed enhancement. Completed CRP’s shall be submitted on-line using the CR submission application on the OGC web site. Submitted CRP’s are catalogued and stored on a publicly accessible site (see <eref type="inline" bibitemid="OGCCRApp" citeas="OGC Change Request Application"/>).</p>

<p id="_1a716885-a0ff-f105-ec35-54c2c6d81dc2">CRP’s are used as the basis for new SWG work items. The SWG will only consider proposed changes and enhancements to an adopted implementation that have been documented using the CRP template.</p>

<title>The Revision Process</title>
<p id="_8ec219bb-f026-e4a6-aafb-ce4de4f33770">The SWG reviews requests for revisions and corrections to a standard.
