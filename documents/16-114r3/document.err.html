<title>Convenience global attributes for data discovery</title>
<p id="_26ebf535-96fd-fb52-3873-fe60673d2480">This sub-section presents global attributes that duplicate information available in other attributes, but in a more convenient manner. For example, the <em>geographic bounding box</em> attributes (<xref target="geographic_bounding_box"/>) duplicate information provided by the <em>geospatial bounds</em> attributes (<xref target="geospatial_bounds"/> to <xref target="temporal_bounds"/>), but may avoid the need to apply an inverse map projection. The geospatial bounds attributes themselves duplicate attributes associated to coordinate variables. But the attributes described in this sub-section make it easier to discover the data. Indeed, all attributes in this sub-section come from the Attribute Convention for Data Discovery (ACDD). By contrast, most attributes in other sections come from the Climate and Forecast (CF) convention.</p>
